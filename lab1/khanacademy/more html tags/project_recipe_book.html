Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 2 tablespoons
Baking powder 1 teaspoon
Salt Pinch
Butter (melted) 2 tablespoons

Step 1: In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

Step 2: In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, and melted butter.

Step 3: Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until smooth.

Step 4: Heat a pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.

Step 5: Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan for each pancake.

Step 6: Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook the other side until golden brown.

Step 7: Serve warm with syrup, fruit, or your favorite toppings.
